Frequently asked
Common contact questions.
What is your minimum order quantity?
We accept from 1 piece for premium items like crystal and plaques. Standard trophy models usually start at 10 pieces, with bulk pricing from 50+.
Do you offer engraving or logo printing?
Yes. We provide laser engraving, UV colour printing, and metal sublimation with your logo, award title, and recipient names.
How quickly can you deliver?
Most orders dispatch in 5–7 working days after design approval. Express 48-hour turnaround is available for select models.
How do payments work?
We support bank transfer, UPI, cards, and cheque for bulk orders. A 50% advance starts production; corporate credit terms are available after the first completed order.
