Who We Are

A recognition brand built on craft, trust, and long-term partnerships.

Legacy Awards began in 2001 with a simple belief: meaningful achievements deserve equally meaningful recognition. What started as a small workshop has grown into a trusted production partner for schools, businesses, sporting bodies, and institutions across India.

Today, our work combines premium materials, thoughtful design, and dependable manufacturing. Whether the project is a one-off executive trophy or a large-scale annual awards order, our goal remains the same - deliver work that feels important, polished, and memorable.

We specialize in blending brass, marble, crystal, wood, and metal finishes into products that look ceremonial, refined, and durable. Every piece is designed to feel worthy of the moment it represents.
