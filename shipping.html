Shipping details

Your order’s delivery should be as reliable as the recognition it represents. Here’s how Legacy Awards handles shipping across India.

Delivery timelines

  • Standard delivery is 5–10 business days depending on your location.
  • Express and rush order options are available for faster turnaround.
  • Custom approval and production timelines are shared before shipping begins.

Packaging and protection

All awards are packed with protective materials, reinforced boxes, and secure internal supports to prevent damage in transit.

Tracking and updates

  • We provide tracking details when your order ships.
  • Delivery notifications are sent by email or WhatsApp.
  • Support is available if there are any carrier delays.

Shipping charges

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout and depend on item size, weight, and delivery location. Large trophy or bulk orders may receive custom shipping rates.

Local pickup option

If you are nearby, we can arrange order pickup from our workshop or showroom. This is ideal for last-minute events and ensures your awards arrive without shipping delay.
