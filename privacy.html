Privacy Policy

How Legacy Awards protects your data and privacy.

We collect only the information needed to serve you, keep it secure, and never share it without your permission. This page explains how we handle customer data for orders, communications, and website use.

Privacy first, always.

We only use personal data to process orders, reply to inquiries, and improve our website experience. Your details are never sold or shared outside of order fulfillment and trusted service partners.

  • Only necessary details are collected.
  • Secure storage with limited access.
  • Data is used only for order and support purposes.

Privacy policy overview

A clear policy helps your customers understand what information you collect, why you collect it, and how you keep it safe. These statements are designed to match the same tone and structure as your storefront pages.

Information we collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number.
  • Shipping and billing addresses for order delivery.
  • Order details, customization requirements, and invoice data.
  • Optional account login credentials if customers register.

How we use it

  • Process orders, prepare quotes, and manage production.
  • Send order updates, delivery notifications, and customer support replies.
  • Improve our site content and product recommendations.
  • Comply with legal and accounting requirements.

Data security

We use secure systems, restricted access, and regular reviews to protect customer information. Personal data is stored only as long as needed for order fulfillment and customer support.

Third-party service providers

We may share data with trusted partners for payment processing, shipping, email communication, and analytics. Those partners are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Customer rights

Customers can request details about the information we hold, ask for corrections, or request deletion of data when it is no longer required. We are committed to supporting privacy requests quickly.
