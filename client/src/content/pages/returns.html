Returns and order adjustments made simple.

Clear returns for non-custom orders.

Custom trophy orders, once approved and manufactured, are generally not returnable. We do offer revisions for artwork, engraving, and production setup before final approval.

✓ Returns accepted for unopened stock items.
⚠️ Custom orders can be revised before production starts.

Return process in three steps

1. Request

Send order details, condition, and reason within 7 days.

2. Review

We inspect and confirm whether the item qualifies for return or revision.

3. Resolution

Refund, replacement, or repair details are shared once review is complete.

Our returns approach

This policy gives clients confidence while also protecting the custom work your business produces.

Standard order returns

  • Stock items may be returned within 7 days if unopened and undamaged.
  • Return requests should include order ID and condition details.
  • Shipping cost for returns is handled by the customer unless the item arrived damaged.
  • Refunds are processed after inspection and may take 5–7 business days.

Custom order revisions

  • Design and engraving changes are managed during proof approval.
  • Once production begins, only material or finishing corrections are accepted.
  • We require clear confirmation for all custom requests before manufacturing.

Damaged or incorrect delivery

If a shipped item arrives damaged or incorrect, notify us within 48 hours. We will arrange a replacement or repair with priority support.

Order cancellation

Cancellations are accepted before production begins. For fully custom pieces, cancellation requests after approval may incur partial charges.

Need help?

If you are unsure whether your order qualifies for return or revision, please contact our support team. We will clarify the next steps and help you keep your event schedule on track.
